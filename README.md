## 📖 About KOReader

KOReader is a free and open-source app for e-ink readers and Android devices. It handles many document types such as PDF, EPUB, and DjVu. It includes features like:

- Smooth text rendering
- Customizable reading settings
- Dictionary support
- Annotation tools

KOReader works on many popular readers, including Kindle, Kobo, PocketBook, and reMarkable.

The **awesome-koreader** list helps users find and install plugins, themes, and tools to make KOReader better.

## 🔧 How to Transfer Plugins and Themes to Your Device

To use a plugin or theme, you usually copy its files to your KOReader device. The process varies by device:

### For Android Devices:
1. Connect your Android device to your PC with a USB cable.
2. Open the device storage folder in File Explorer.
3. Find the KOReader folder (usually under `Android/data` or similar).
4. Copy the plugin or theme files into their proper folders as explained in the plugin’s instructions.
5. Disconnect safely and restart KOReader on your device.

### For Kindle or Kobo:
1. Connect the e-ink reader to your PC with a USB cable.
2. Open the device storage drive shown in File Explorer.
3. Look for a `koreader` or `plugins` folder.
4. Copy the plugin or theme files into the appropriate folders.
5. Eject the device safely and restart KOReader.

---

## 🔍 How to Use Plugins and Themes in KOReader

1. Open KOReader on your device.
2. Navigate to the **Plugins** or **Themes** section in the settings menu.
3. Enable or select the new plugin or theme you transferred.
4. Follow any instructions included with the plugin for setup or configuration.

---

## 🛠 Troubleshooting Tips

- If a plugin doesn’t show up, check if you placed the files in the correct folder.
- Restart KOReader after installing new plugins or themes.
- Some plugins may require a certain KOReader version. Check their documentation.
- If KOReader crashes, remove the last plugin you added and try again.
